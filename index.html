BEST CARTOONS OF ALL-TIME!

The Simpsons

The Simpsons is an American animated sitcom created by Matt Groening. The show is set in the fictional town of Springfield and parodies American culture and society, television, and the human condition.

Family Guy

Family Guy is an American animated sitcom created by Seth MacFarlane. The show is set in the fictional city of Quahog, Rhode Island, and exhibits much of its humor in the form of metafictional cutaway gags that often lampoon American culture.
